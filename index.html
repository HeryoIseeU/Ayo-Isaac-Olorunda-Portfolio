Case Study

A mobile app prototype designed to help residents of Samaru report electrical issues, check power schedules, and contact local electricity providers ‚Äì all in one place. Created during the ALX Teamwork Sprint using human-centered design principles.

Problem

The Samaru Electricity Mobile App was created to solve the problem of poor communication between residents and electricity providers. People in Samaru often face power outages without warning, have no clear way to report faults, and struggle to get updates or support. This project aimed to make access to electricity services easier, faster, and more transparent through a user-friendly mobile app.

Solution

  • A mobile app designed for easy use by all residents
  • Allows users to report power outages and electrical faults instantly
  • Enables users to pay electricity bills on time to avoid disconnection
  • Improves communication between residents and electricity providers
  • Built using human-centered design principles to ensure user needs are met
